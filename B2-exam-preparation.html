📋 RULES quick ref

summary
  • 1 Simple Past – main story actions
  • 2 Past Continuous – background / simultaneous actions
  • 3 Past Perfect – earlier completed action (before main event)
  • 4 Past Perfect Continuous – duration / repeated actions before a past moment
  • ⚠️ Stative verbs (know, like, be) – never continuous
